Meet Amy Winehouse.

Amy Winehouse was a legendary singer-songwriter that created timeless music with her unique and recognizable voice.

Consider someone embodying a larger than life personality, equipt with a whiskey-soaked voice infused with so much soul and rhythm that it could move a brass statue. That was Amy Winehouse. In the beginning while performing at small jazz venues, she was kept a recording industry secret. However in October 2008 when her album 'Back to Black' was released, she was a secret no more. Topping charts world wide, she would prove to be one of the greatest voices in Soul. Even though she's gone, Amy leaves us with the memory of that powerful whiskey-soaked voice and some of the most powerful music that will ever move you.
